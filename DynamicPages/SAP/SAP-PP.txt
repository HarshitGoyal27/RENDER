Brief Job Advertisement for SAP PP Developer at SkillCapital

SkillCapital, a leading provider of SAP solutions, is seeking a highly skilled and experienced SAP PP Developer to join our team. The successful candidate will have a proven track record of success in implementing and supporting SAP PP modules, and will be responsible for the development, testing, and deployment of SAP PP solutions.

Roles and Responsibilities for SAP PP Developer

1. Design, develop, test, and implement SAP PP modules.
2. Analyze and resolve PP-related issues and provide technical support to users.
3. Work closely with cross-functional teams to ensure successful project delivery.
4. Stay up-to-date with the latest SAP PP developments and best practices.
5. Provide training and support to users on SAP PP functionality.
6. Document and maintain SAP PP configurations and processes.

Job Requirements for SAP PP Developer

1. Minimum experience of 3 years or a highly skilled fresher in SAP PP.
2. Strong understanding of SAP PP concepts and processes.
3. Excellent ABAP programming skills.
4. Experience in working with SAP PP interfaces and integrations.
5. Good understanding of SD, MM, and QM modules.
6. Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
7. Excellent communication and teamwork skills.

Preferred Skills for SAP PP Developer

1. Experience in working with SAP S/4HANA.
2. Knowledge of SAP Fiori and SAPUI5.
3. Experience in working with SAP Leonardo IoT and SAP Analytics Cloud.
4. Experience in working with SAP PP add-ons and third-party tools.
5. Knowledge of Lean Manufacturing and Six Sigma principles.
6. Certification in SAP PP or related areas.
